About Us

TripAid is an all-in-one travel app that provides an online booking platform, web mapping platform, travel planner, and also a social media platform for user's to share their travel stories or engage with our TripAid Community!

Features

TripFeed

Share or view other user's travel itineraries, blogs, and posts all in one feed!

Map

Discover new destinations and also walking routes curated just for you!

Booking

Book and pay for all your attractions, accomodations, restaurants, and transporations all under one app!

Forum

A place where you can ask questions, share your experiences, discuss mutual interest, and connect with our TripAid community!
